About NUG

National Unity Government of Myanmar

The Interim National Unity Government of the Republic of the Union of Myanmar is the only and legitimate Government of the Republic of the Union of Myanmar formed by the Committee Representing Pyidaungsu Hluttaw CRPH with the authority bestowed by the Peoples mandate of the all parties democractic election held in 2020 in discussion with National Unity Consultative Council.
